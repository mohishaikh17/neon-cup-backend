Organization History

Founded by the legendary **Naman "Mortal" Mathur**, Soul Esports is widely considered the most beloved e-sports organization in India. The team rose to fame in 2019, winning back-to-back major titles and building an unprecedented legacy of sportsmanship and competitive excellence.

Team Soul represents more than just a squad; it is a movement that helped bring mobile gaming into the Indian mainstream. Their victory at BMPS Season 1 cemented their status as the kings of the scene, showcasing a perfect blend of tactical depth and individual brilliance.

Currently operating under the banner of **Team iQOO Soul**, the organization continues to be a dominant force in BGMI. With a philosophy centered on discipline and fan engagement, Soul remains the heartbeat of the Indian gaming community.
