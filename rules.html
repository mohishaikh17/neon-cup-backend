Tournament Rulebook

1. General Rules & Fair Play

  • All players must maintain a sportsmanlike conduct. Toxicity, harassment, or cheating will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Only mobile devices are allowed. Emulators are strictly prohibited.
  • Teams must consist of 4 players. No solo or duo entries in the squad tournament.
  • Player and Team names must be appropriate and not contain offensive language.

2. Scoring System

  • Placement Points: Points are awarded based on a team's final ranking in each match.
  • Kill Points: Each kill is worth 1 point.
  • The final score is the sum of Placement Points and Kill Points.

3. Match Procedure

  • Room ID and Password will be shared with the team captain via a designated WhatsApp/Discord group 15 minutes before the match.
  • Teams must be in the lobby at least 5 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • If a player disconnects, the team must continue playing with the remaining members. Rematches will only be considered in the case of a major server-wide failure.
