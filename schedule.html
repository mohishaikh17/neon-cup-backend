Tournament Roadmap

Stage 1: Online Qualifiers

Date: October 4th - 5th, 2025

Time: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM IST

Format: 100 registered teams will be divided into 5 groups. Each group plays 3 matches. Top 4 teams from each group advance.

Maps: Erangel, Miramar, Sanhok

Stage 2: Quarter-Finals

Date: October 11th, 2025

Time: 7:00 PM - 10:00 PM IST

Format: The 20 qualifying teams will play 4 matches. Top 12 teams advance to the Semi-Finals.

Maps: Erangel, Miramar, Erangel, Vikendi

Stage 3: Semi-Finals

Date: October 18th, 2025

Time: 7:00 PM - 10:00 PM IST

Format: The top 12 teams battle it out in 5 matches. Top 8 secure their spot in the Grand Finals.

Maps: Erangel, Miramar, Sanhok, Vikendi, Erangel

Stage 4: The Grand Finals

Date: October 25th, 2025

Time: 6:00 PM onwards

Format: The final 8 teams will play a Best-of-5 series to determine the champions of the Neon Cup 2025!

Maps: Erangel, Miramar, Erangel, Miramar, Erangel
