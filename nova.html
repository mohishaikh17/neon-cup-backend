Organization History

Based in Hong Kong and competing in the ultra-competitive Peacekeeper Elite League (PEL) in China, **Nova Esports** is the most successful PUBG Mobile organization in existence. They are the only team to have achieved back-to-back world titles, winning both PMGC 2020 and PMGC 2021.

Nova is known for its "Imperial" playstyle—dominating the center of the zone and utilizing superior firepower to gatekeep entire lobbies. Their systematic approach to the game has set the gold standard for tactical coordination and consistency on the global stage.

With legends like Paraboy and Order on their roster, Nova Esports has built a dynasty that transcends regions, commanding a global fanbase and a trophy cabinet that remains unmatched by any other squad.
