Organization History

Formerly known as **Orange Rock**, OG Esports is one of the oldest and most respected names in the Indian battle royale circuit. They are famously remembered for their incredible run in the PMWL (PUBG Mobile World League) East 2020, where they secured a historic 2nd place, proving that Indian teams could stand shoulder-to-shoulder with global giants.

The organization has always been defined by its strategic patience and calculated aggression. After a brief hiatus and rebranding, OG remains a gatekeeper of the competitive scene, consistently producing talent that excels under high-pressure final scenarios.

With a legacy rooted in the "Golden Era" of mobile e-sports, OG Esports continues to evolve, adapting to the shifting BGMI meta while maintaining their signature orange identity.
